# Global Super Store Sales Report Dashboard

## Overview

The **Global Super Store Sales Report Dashboard** is a comprehensive business intelligence tool designed to provide real-time insights into sales performance, profitability, and operational metrics across multiple dimensions. This dashboard enables executives, managers, and analysts to make data-driven decisions by visualizing key performance indicators (KPIs) and detailed analytics across geographic regions, product categories, and customer segments.

## Dashboard Highlights

### Core Metrics
- **Total Sales**: $12.64M - Overall revenue generated
- **Total Profit**: $1.47M - Net profit across all operations
- **Total Orders**: 25K - Volume of transactions processed
- **Average Delivery Days**: 3.97 - Average time to delivery (operational efficiency metric)
- **Total Quantity Sold**: 178K units - Volume of products distributed

### Time Period Coverage
The dashboard analyzes data spanning 2011-2014, allowing for year-over-year comparisons and trend analysis.

## Glossary

- **Total Sales**: Sum of all revenue from product sales
- **Total Profit**: Revenue minus all operational costs
- **Average Delivery Days**: Mean time from order placement to delivery
- **Total Quantity Sold**: Total number of units sold across all products
- **Segment**: Customer classification (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)
- **Ship Mode**: Method of product delivery (Standard, Second Class, First Class)
